# üß† AI Schema Use Guide for Replit Deployment (2025+)

This guide outlines the **minimum required** and **recommended (best to have)** Schema.org types and properties for content optimized for AI visibility ‚Äî including Google SGE, Bing Copilot, Perplexity, ChatGPT, Claude, and Meta AI.

## ‚úÖ Minimum Required Schema (Must-Have for AI Optimization)

These schemas **must be implemented** to meet the foundational AI and SEO standards:

### 1. `BlogPosting` (or `Article`)

* `@type`: `BlogPosting`
* `headline`
* `description`
* `author` ‚Üí `Person`
* `publisher` ‚Üí `Organization`
* `mainEntityOfPage`
* `datePublished`
* `dateModified`
* `image`
* `url`

### 2. `BreadcrumbList`

* Clarifies the content hierarchy to AI engines
* `@type`: `BreadcrumbList`
* `itemListElement` (array of `ListItem`s with name & URL)

### 3. `Organization`

* Defines your brand for trust and E-A-T
* `@type`: `Organization`
* `name`
* `logo`
* `url`
* `sameAs` (link to social profiles)

### 4. `Person` (for Author)

* Attached to the `author` of `BlogPosting`
* `@type`: `Person`
* `name`
* `jobTitle`
* `sameAs`

### 5. `FAQPage` (if FAQs are present)

* `@type`: `FAQPage`
* Contains list of `Question` + `acceptedAnswer` blocks

### 6. `WebPage`

* `@type`: `WebPage`
* `name`
* `description`
* `inLanguage`
* `isPartOf`

---

## üöÄ Recommended Schema (Best for AI Discovery & Voice Support)

### 7. `HowTo`

* For step-based content (guides, tutorials)
* `@type`: `HowTo`
* `name`
* `step` (array of `HowToStep`s)
* Optional: `image`, `tool`, `estimatedCost`

### 8. `VideoObject` (if video is embedded)

* `@type`: `VideoObject`
* `name`
* `description`
* `uploadDate`
* `thumbnailUrl`
* `transcript`

### 9. `Speakable` (experimental, Google-only)

* Used for voice assistants to read aloud summary sections
* `@type`: `SpeakableSpecification`
* `xpath` or `cssSelector`

### 10. `QAPage` or `Question`

* If you're covering a single-question prompt or using AI prompt-style headings
* `@type`: `QAPage`
* Contains a `Question` object + `acceptedAnswer`

### 11. `Product` + `Review` + `AggregateRating`

* For product or tool reviews and SaaS coverage
* `@type`: `Product`, `Review`, `AggregateRating`
* `name`, `reviewBody`, `reviewRating`, `ratingValue`

### 12. `Course` or `LearningResource`

* If the content is educational or training-based
* `@type`: `Course` or `LearningResource`
* `name`, `description`, `provider`, `educationalLevel`

## üß© Integration Tips for Replit & Static Deployments

* Use `<script type="application/ld+json">` inside your HTML `<head>` or inline per section.
* Use JSON-LD formatting exclusively ‚Äî it's preferred by all major AI engines.
* If using Markdown, inject schemas via an HTML component block.
* Auto-generate with tools like [Schema.dev](https://schema.dev/) or [Merkle‚Äôs Schema Generator](https://technicalseo.com/tools/schema-markup-generator/).
